Analyzing the relationship between personality traits and drug consumption

Posted on: Tue 11 July 2017

Tags: #python, #scikit-learn, #pandas, #machine-learning

Substance abuse is one of the biggest unsolved problems of our times. Several strategies, including abstinence, harm reduction, psychotherapy and medication have been used to tackle this problem. I feel that understanding is a crucial step in solving a social issue. In this post, I try to take a small step towards this by analyzing the relationship between drug consumption and personality traits of a person.

The data used is from the UCI database available (https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Drug+consumption+%28quantified%29)[here].
